Ever. Expending. Horizons.
A learning mindset is essential for a UX designer. To excel in this field, one must be open to continuous learning and improvement. As technology and user behavior evolve, so must a UX designer's skills and knowledge. It also involves being open to constructive criticism, being comfortable with ambiguity, and recognizing that failures are opportunities for growth and improvement.
